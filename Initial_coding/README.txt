What is in these folders?


*******COMMS_BOUNCE_BACK***

This will sit on an ATmega48 MCU and will bounce back data sent to it.
It only needs to interface with the comms module, SPI



*******Comms_GUI***

Sits on the PC and trancieves data from the copter. Graphs data and creates log files




*******UART_2_SPI***

This will sit on an ATmega48 MCU and will take data from the UART (PC) and put it on
the comms module (SPI)
